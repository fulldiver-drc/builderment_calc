Builderment Helper

A simple github.io project I decided to make as an initial foray into the platform. The UI elements are styled after a low-code platform that I already use, but I miss writing actual code, so here I am (I get that html and css isn't actual coding, but I'll take what I can get, lol).
Builderment just happens to be a game I'm playing at the moment, so I figured why not?

Included in this project are:

  • A calculator to determine the required resources and facilities to produce any item, accessible here or via the Production Calculator link in the menu
  • A list of all items and their details, accessible here or via the Items link in the menu bar


Enjoy.
